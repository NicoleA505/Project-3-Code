Films

About Time

A 2013 British romantic comedy-drama film about a young man with the ability to time travel who tries to change his past in hopes of improving his future.

Jurassic Park

From 1993 is about John Hammond, a millionaire/billionaire who has built a zoo/theme park with dinosaurs by cloning them back to life from DNA found in ancient mosquito blood trapped in amber.

Cast Away

Film from 2000, depicting a FedEx employee marooned on an uninhabited island after his plane crashes in the South Pacific and his attempts to survive on the island using remnants of his plane's cargo

Mary Poppins

Stars Julie Andrews in her feature film debut as Mary Poppins, who visits a dysfunctional family in London and employs her unique brand of lifestyle to improve the family's dynamic.

Finding Nemo

A clown fish named Marlin lives in the Great Barrier Reef and loses his son, Nemo, after he ventures into the open sea, despite his father's constant warnings about many of the ocean's dangers.

Love, Actually

2003 Christmas-themed romantic comedy which delves into different aspects of love as shown through ten separate stories involving a wide variety of individuals, many of whom are shown to be interlinked as the tales progress.

Festival News

Excitement rises over Brooklyn's upcoming film festival

July 4, 2019

A recent local poll showed that citizens living in Brooklyn and Manhatten are greatly looking forward to the upcoming film festival at Brooklyn Bridge Park. Although the venue can only accomodate for around 5,000 people per day, it's looking like a great deal more of the population is interested in attending. Those interested should register and sign up for the festival as soon as possible to secure their place!

Brooklyn Film Festival puts together it's schedule

Feb 15, 2019

The Brooklyn Outdoor Film Festival has finally created it's list of films as well as what times they will be playing at. The organizers of the festival spent several weeks discussing with studios and weighing the options that were provided them. They feel their final line-up provides a good mix for all festival-goers no matter their age.

Film Festival is confirmed at Brooklyn Bridge Park!

Nov 26, 2018

After many weeks of negotiations and bureacracy, the City of Brooklyn has finally given the go-ahead for the Brooklyn Outdoor Film Festival to be held this year at Brooklyn Bridge Park. The venue is set to be this summer between August 5 - 8 from 6pm to Midnight.
